This is what the Hollywood backlash to #MeToo looks like
Their latest profile of a fallen celebrity is a rather sympathetic interview with Jeffrey Tambor, the “Transparent” actor accused of sexually harassing his personal assistant and a “Transparent” costar – both of whom are transgender. The piece comes off as if it were asking, “Don’t you remember how funny and dedicated a performer Tambor can be? Or how important the show is for trans visibility? He’s sorry now. He’s mourning the loss of his endearing character, Maura. Look at these somber photos of the ‘Arrested Development’ star.“
