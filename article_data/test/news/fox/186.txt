Rep. Matt Gaetz (R-FL): John Kerry Is Undermining Our Work
Play Audio Clip Listen to audio clip.

Shortly following President Trump's announcement that the United States would be withdrawing from the Iran Nuclear Deal, Todd Starnes got a reaction from Rep. Matt Gaetz (R-FL). The Congressman had harsh words for former Secretary of State John Kerry.

Rep. Gaetz said, "John Kerry is undermining our work. John Kerry is out meeting with Iranian officials in anticipation of the President doing precisely what he said he was going to do. John Kerry needs to retire."
