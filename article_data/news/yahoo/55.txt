An Allegiant Air flight made an emergency landing in Asheville, North Carolina on Sunday after airline crew noticed an “electrical smell,” the airliner confirmed to Fox News. “The crew noticed a light electrical smell in flight, which persisted for several minutes to where they decided to divert in order to have maintenance check it out, out of an abundance of caution,” an airline representative said in a statement to Fox News. The flight departed from Indianapolis, ID, for Myrtle Beach, SC, at 3 pm. The pilot made the emergency landing into Asheville Regional Airport at 4:15 pm, according to Flight Aware.
