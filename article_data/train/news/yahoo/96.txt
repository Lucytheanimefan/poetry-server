May 10 (UPI) -- A helicopter tour company captured video of the scary moment a kayaker fell into the water with a great white shark off the California coast. A video posted to YouTube by Specialized Helicopter Tours shows Giancarlo Thomae falling into the water off the Santa Cruz coast while trying to retrieve his glasses. The video shows a great white shark investigating Thomae by swimming around him in a wide circle. Thomae is able to safely make it back to his kayak without any direct confrontation with the predator.
