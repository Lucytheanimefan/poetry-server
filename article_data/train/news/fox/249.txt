Tony Perkins: President Trump stands up for religious liberty
Play Audio Clip Listen to audio clip.

Todd Starnes spoke with Family Research Council President Tony Perkins about the executive order President Trump signed establishing a White House Faith and Opportunity Initiative, as well as the celebration of the National Day of Prayer at the White House, which Perkins attended.

"The hundred-eighty-degree turn that we've been seeing in many agencies when it comes to rights of conscience and faith is absolutely remarkable," Perkins said. He then said, "And today with his executive order of the Faith Initiative, that's only going to take it to the next step. This is going to be huge."
