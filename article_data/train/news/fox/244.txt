FOX News Radio's National Correspondent Jared Halpern along with a team of FOX News reporters & contributors look at the week that was inside the beltway.

THIS WEEK:

President Trump's newest legal adviser, Rudy Giuliani, appeared to contradict the president when he told FOX's Sean Hannity that he was aware of the $130,000 payment to Stormy Daniels. FOX's White House Correspondent Jon Decker discusses the latest.

This week, asylum seekers, who traveled in a caravan from Central America, were allowed into the U.S. to plead their case for asylum. FOX's West Coast Correspondent Jessica Rosenthal spoke to some organizers who work with refugees.

On Tuesday night, FOX News hosted a debate in Morgantown, West Virginia between the three top Republican candidates hoping to flip the states U.S. Senate seat, which is currently held by Democrat Joe Manchin. Jared was in Morgantown and chatted with Mountaineer state voters.

At a recognition ceremony this week at the White House, Mandy Manning was awarded as the National Teacher of the Year, for her work with refugee and immigrant students in Spokane, Washington. Manning joined the podcast to discuss her work.
