Behind the Cover: Children of the Opioid Epidemic
Jake Silverstein, editor in chief: “It can be difficult to take a great photograph of a subject who does not want her identity revealed. But this shot is arresting, the perfect image for Jennifer Egan’s article about opioids and pregnancy. The way this woman is bent over her innocent baby, embracing and protecting her while at the same time burying her face in the child’s back — it seemed to us to perfectly capture the feelings of agony, resilience and hope that the mothers in Egan’s article experience.”
